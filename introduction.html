welcome to solitude


Step 1:

Think of something that inspires you, and write it down in the stream input box in the top of the bar on your left.


Step 2:

Hit enter and watch the feed stream twitter updates that may inspire you! See a tweet that inspires you? Click it to pin it down before it escapes.


Step 3:

Start writing!
